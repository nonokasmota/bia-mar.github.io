Flower Food Compendium

What is this?

To be fair we are pretty confused too.

We are two friends who like to eat flowers, just like the cows. Flowers are so pretty we get the munchies, what can we say?

We started this project as a way to keep track of the recipes we wanted to try.

We also made this as a way to share some nuggets of culture with others, who just like us, eat flowers.

"i like dem flowers better in my stomach than on the ground" - probably Ana at some point

All these recipes have been foraged, stolen. Just like the flowers they use.
